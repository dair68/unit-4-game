Crystal Collector!


Instructions

In this game, the goal is to get your score to match a certain number by clicking on crystals.

Click on any of the four crystals to add points to your score. Each crystal grants a specific amount of points. If your score matches the target number, you win. If it exceeds the target number, you lose.

Since the value of each crystal differs from game to game, you'll only know how many points a crystal gives once you click on it. The target number also changes every game.
